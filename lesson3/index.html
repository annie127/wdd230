Site Name

Idaho Weather Live

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide vital information for Idaho cities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. This site contains weather information, latest events and sporting news in the said cities. There is a 5-day weather forecast that helps audience plan their activities around the said cities. The storm center page lets audience report the storms that are currently happening in their area. There is a gallery page that shows beautiful photos from various parts of Idaho. We want our audience to be able to reach us so our contact information, location map and social media pages are all on this website.
