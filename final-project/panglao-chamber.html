Our Purpose

The purpose of Panglao Chamber is to serve the interests of businesses around Panglao through the cooperation of our members in promoting our long-term objectives. We are focused on contributing to the economic and civic development of the town.

Our main responsibility is to help further the interests of small businesses in Panglao by doing range of activities from charitable works to hosting events for the betterment of the community. We believe we can achieve this goal if we work close together.

Panglao Chamber is your valuable partner in achieving your business goals.

Upcoming Events

Jan 30
1pm to 5pm

Sinulog sa Panglao

Sinulog sa Panglao is a festive event which happens every 4th week of January. It is patterned from a festival in Cebu which is also called Sinulog. This event is participated by people from different sectors competing with each other and showcasing the town’s culture through colorful costumes and dances.

July 10
6am to 2pm

Ironman 5150 Triathlon

The World Triathlon Corporation, owners of the Ironman and Ironman 70.3 races started the 5i50 series in 2011 in the United States. The 5150 Series is the largest international distance triathlon series in the world and will be the first non-drafting international race series of its kind, offering a competitive platform for professional and age group athletes. The 5150 Series consists of a 1.5K-swim, 40K- bike and 10k-run.

July 20
8am to 12noon

Dragon Boat Festival

In this event, local paddlers show their strength, endurance and teamwork through a synchronized 300 meters of paddling. Participated by various paddlers from all over the country, this event sees them padding through Dauis and Tagbilaran Strait. The currents and waters of the strait make this event an exciting and must-see event.

Aug 28
1pm to 5pm

Hudyaka sa Panglao

Hudyaka sa Panglao is the oldest and most celebrated event in Panglao. It happens every 28th day of August. Just like the Sinulog festival, Hudyaka sa Panglao also showcased the town’s culture and tradition. It is participated by the ten different barangays (communities) of Panglao. They dance along the town’s street with their colorful costumes and lively music.
